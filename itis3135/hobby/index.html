When

Best times: weekend mornings and cool evenings. Big events happen more in spring and fall.

  • Sat/Sun drives
  • Weeknight DIY tasks
  • Spring/Fall = most meets

Where

I look for smooth roads, safe pull-offs, and good photo spots. Local tracks are great for learning.

  • Scenic routes / parkways
  • Cars & Coffee lots
  • Beginner track days

How

Start small and safe: set a budget, maintain the car, and choose simple, reversible mods.

  1. Maintenance first (fluids, brakes, tires)
  2. Plan your goals (daily, track, show)
  3. Research before modding

Why

I like the engineering, the driving feel, and the community. Cars are a hands-on way to learn and make friends.

  • Mechanical learning
  • Personal expression
  • Memorable drives
