Welcome to C3

The 49ers Car Culture Club is a friendly place for car fans of all kinds. It does not matter if you daily drive a stock commuter or have a full build. Everyone is welcome.

We host meets, cruises, workshops, and photo days during the semester. Check the Events page for dates and times.

What is C3?

C3 is built around six simple questions: who, what, where, when, why, and how.

Who

UNC Charlotte students, alumni, and friends who enjoy cars and car culture.

What

Car meets, cruises, tech nights, and simple maintenance workshops.

Where

Mostly on campus near the Student Union and CRI Deck, plus local photo spots.

When

We aim for at least one event per month during the fall and spring semesters.

Why

To build a safe, relaxed community around cars and driving.

How

Fill out the Join form, then come to a meet and say hello. That is it.
