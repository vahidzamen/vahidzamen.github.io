Welcome to C3

The 49ers Car Culture Club (C3) is a student-run organization for anyone who loves cars— whether you own a project car, daily drive a stock commuter, or just love looking at builds on Instagram. We host meetups, cruises, workshops, and social events throughout the semester.

Our mission is to create a safe, inclusive, and fun space for car fans of all backgrounds and experience levels to connect, learn, and share their passion.

Who

UNC Charlotte students, alumni, and friends who love cars, motorsports, and design.

What

Meets, cruises, tech nights, detail & photo tips, and collaborative projects.

Where

Mostly on campus near the Student Union, plus local photo spots and cruise routes.

When

We host at least one event per month during the semester. Check the Events page for dates.

Why

To build community, help people learn, and give everyone a welcoming space to enjoy car culture.

How

Fill out the form on the Join page, then show up to a meetup. That’s it.
