Welcome to C3

The 49ers Car Culture Club is a friendly place for car fans of all kinds. It does not matter if you daily a stock commuter, have a full build, or just like looking at cars — everyone is welcome.

We host meets, cruises, workshops, and photo days during the semester. Check the Events page for dates and times, then come hang out with other people who get just as excited about cars as you do.

What is C3?

C3 is built around six simple questions: who, what, where, when, why, and how. Together, they explain what our club is all about.

Who

UNC Charlotte students, alumni, and friends who enjoy cars and car culture — from daily drivers to full builds.

What

Casual meets, cruises, tech nights, simple maintenance workshops, and plenty of time to talk cars.

Where

Mostly on campus near the Student Union and CRI Deck, plus local photo spots around Charlotte.

When

We aim for at least one event per month during the fall and spring semesters, with extra meets when the weather is nice.

Why

To build a safe, relaxed community around cars and driving — no ego, no pressure, just people who love cars.

How

Fill out the Join form, follow our announcements, then come to a meet and say hello. That is it.
