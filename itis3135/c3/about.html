Our Mission

C3 exists to bring car enthusiasts together in a positive way. We encourage safe driving, respect for others, and a welcoming space for people who are new to cars.

The club mixes casual social events with simple learning activities, like basic maintenance days and photo walks.

Who We Are

Our members are a mix of engineering students, business majors, commuters, and dorm residents. Some wrench on their cars every weekend. Others just enjoy looking at builds and talking about future projects.

C3 works with other campus groups for charity events and shared meets to show that car culture can be safe, kind, and fun.
