Our Mission

The 49ers Car Culture Club (C3) exists to bring together UNC Charlotte students who share a love of cars, whether that means drifting, detailing, engineering, or photography.

We focus on community, safety, and respect—no ego, no gatekeeping. C3 is a place where someone with their first car can park next to a fully built project and feel equally welcome.

Who We Are

We’re a mix of engineering students, designers, business majors, commuters, and dorm residents. Some members wrench on their cars every weekend; others just love going to meets and talking about dream builds.

C3 collaborates with other campus organizations for charity cruises, workshops, and social events, helping show that car culture can be positive, safe, and inclusive.
