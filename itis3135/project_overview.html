Project Overview — 49ers Car Culture Club (C3)

Client & Project Category

Client Category: Student group at UNCC
Project Category: Group site encouraging participation and communication

1) Application Purpose

The 49ers Car Culture Club (C3) website will promote club activities, display event media, highlight upcoming meets, and serve as an interest point for new members.

2) Intended Users

  • Prospective members
  • Active members
  • Club officers
  • Partner organizations and sponsors

3) Content Overview

  • About / Mission
  • Events
  • Gallery
  • Join / Contact Form
  • Resources (optional)
