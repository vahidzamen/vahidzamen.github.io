Project Overview — 49ers Car Culture Club (C3)

Client & Project Category

Client Category (Step 01): Student group at UNCC
Project Category (Step 02): Group site that encourages participation & communication

1) Application Purpose

The 49ers Car Culture Club (C3) site organizes events (meets, cruises, tech nights), recruits members, and shares media. It improves visibility, centralizes announcements, and offers light interactivity (gallery, FAQs, event highlights, and a read-only events list).

2) Intended Users

  • Prospective members: basics, how to join, upcoming events
  • Active members: dates, locations, quick links to events and gallery
  • Officers: publish schedules & rules (content provided)
  • Partners / Sponsors: view past events & contact info

3) Content Overview

  • About / mission, officer bios, safety guidelines
  • Events list and highlights
  • Gallery with accessible image grid
  • Join / Contact interest form + campus map
  • Resources (optional): policies & useful links

7) Required Pages & Navigation

  • About (about.html) — mission, officer bios, safety rules
  • Events (events.html) — list of upcoming/past events (read-only)
  • Gallery (gallery.html) — interactive image grid / lightbox
  • Join & Contact (join.html) — interest form (basic validation) and map
  • Optional: Resources (resources.html) — policies & links

The production project will live in its own folder (e.g., project-c3/) with its own HTML/CSS/JS/assets. This overview page stays in the class site with shared header/footer includes.

8) Page Design Details

Home (index.html)
  • Purpose: Welcome / value prop; highlight next event.
  • Audience: Everyone.
  • Content: Hero, brief mission, “Next Event” card, 3-image preview, Join CTA.
  • Inputs: None.
  • Controls: Links to Events & Join.
  • Actions: “Details” opens Events page or a simple modal.
About (about.html)
  • Purpose: Mission, expectations, officer bios.
  • Audience: Prospective members & partners.
  • Inputs: None.
  • Controls / Actions: Simple navigation to Events / Join pages.
Events (events.html)
  • Purpose: Central events list.
  • Audience: Current and potential members.
  • Inputs: None (read-only for this project).
  • Actions: Users can scan dates / locations and follow links to Join.
Gallery (gallery.html)
  • Purpose: Showcase photos from meets and cruises.
  • Audience: Everyone; especially prospects & sponsors.
  • Inputs: None.
  • Actions: Click thumbnails to view larger images (simple lightbox or modal).
Join & Contact (join.html)
  • Purpose: Let visitors express interest and contact officers.
  • Audience: Prospective members & sponsors.
  • Inputs: Name, email, role (student / alumni / partner), message.
  • Actions: Basic client-side validation; form submits to a placeholder endpoint or mailto link.
