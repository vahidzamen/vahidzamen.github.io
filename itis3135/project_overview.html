Project Overview — 49ers Car Culture Club (C3)

Client & Project Category

Client Category: Student organization at UNCC
Project Category: Club website for participation, communication, and visibility

1) Application Purpose

The 49ers Car Culture Club (C3) website serves to organize events, attract new members, showcase media from past meets, and centralize communication between officers and members.

2) Intended Users

  • Prospective members
  • Active members
  • Club officers
  • Partners / sponsors

3) Content Overview

  • About / Mission
  • Event Listings
  • Photo Gallery
  • Join / Contact
  • Resources (optional)
