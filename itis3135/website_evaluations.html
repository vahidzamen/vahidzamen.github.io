Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple’s website is sleek and modern, showcasing products with clean visuals and a minimalist approach.

  • Contrast: Excellent use of white space, dark text, and bold product images.
  • Repetition: Consistent fonts, navigation bar, and layout across pages.
  • Alignment: Content is grid-based and perfectly aligned, making it easy to scan.
  • Proximity: Related items (e.g., product details, pricing) are grouped logically.

Ling's Cars

This site is intentionally chaotic, with clashing visuals, animations, and distracting content everywhere.

  • Contrast: Overuse of bright, clashing colors makes it hard to focus.
  • Repetition: Little consistency; too many different fonts, colors, and styles.
  • Alignment: Elements feel scattered without clear alignment, producing “visual vomit.”
  • Proximity: Related items are often far apart, while unrelated content is crammed together.
